Bio

I am currently working with Machine Learning (ML) team at Egregore Labs as Software Developer - Machine Learning, where I am mainly focus on Computer Vision and NLP.

I conduct research in the field of Deep Learning. more specifically, my areas of interests lies at the intersection of computer vision and natural language processing. Besides, I am also exploring certain areas of Reinforcement Learning, particularly off policy learning.

I was a visiting research scholar at AI-NLP-ML Lab (IIT Patna) advised by Prof. Asif Ekbal and Prof. Pushpak Bhattacharyya . My work was focused on visual dialog, and representation learning. I graduated from the International Institute of Information Technology (IIIT) Bhubaneswar in 2019, majors in Electrical and Electronics Engineering and minor specialization in Computer Science. I was fortunate to work with Prof. Rakesh Balabantaray on NLP and Information Retrieval.
